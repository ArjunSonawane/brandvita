    About Us

    We are a creative agency

    Passionate about designing unique brands and delivering strategic solutions that make your
    business stand out and connect with your audience.

    Formed in 1980, BrandVita carries forward a rich legacy rooted in the Abbasi family's deep expertise in advertising, a tradition spanning nearly five decades. Our journey is built on a strong foundation of creativity, innovation, and an unwavering commitment to impactful brand communication.

    The origins of this legacy can be traced back to Akhtar Abbasi,, a visionary and a pioneer in outdoor advertising in India. His passion and foresight led to the establishment of National Advertisers in 1982. It all began with a single yet iconic hoarding in the city of Kanpur, proudly displaying 'Thums Up'—a milestone that marked the beginning of an era in out-of-home advertising.

    Over the years, this pioneering spirit has shaped BrandVita into a dynamic force in the industry, evolving with changing trends while staying true to its core values of excellence and innovation. Today, we continue to push boundaries, helping brands craft compelling stories and establish meaningful connections with their audiences.

    Success
    story

    Once upon a time, in the bustling city of Kanpur filled with aspiring businesses, there was a small advertising agency called National Advertisers. The agency was founded the passionate entrepreneur, Akhtar Abbasi, who had a vision to help businesses grow by crafting innovative and engaging advertising campaigns.

    As they followed their roadmap to success, National Advertising slowly began to gain traction. Over time, National Advertisers evolved and grew with clients like Hindustan Lever, ITC, TATA, Thums UP (Parle group) Eveready etc.

    After rebranding themselves as BrandVita and the next generation taking control, They expanded their team, taking on talented designers, copywriters, and strategists who shared their passion for advertising. They also embraced new technologies and trends, ensuring their campaigns remained fresh and relevant.

    Today BrandVita is working with clients across various industries, from retail to healthcare, and their reputation as a leading advertising agency continues to soar.

    Currently, they operate with 15+ top-notch cients serving them all the scientific solutions in Advertising, media, and Public Relations.

    But despite their success, they never lost sight of their core values. They remained committed to fostering long-term relationships with their clients and partners, continuously improving their skills, and adapting to the ever-changing advertising landscape.

    Proud
    Moments

    It was the year 2006, when the chief promoter of BrandVita, Mukhtarul Hasan Abbasi (the eldest son of Akhtar Abbasi) added print and electronic media solutions in the portfolio. Clients like Max Fashion, V-Mart Retail, Linen Club (Aditya Birla group), Aakash Institute, Integral University, Walmart-Best Price, Tata Steel followed in. Currently BrandVita Serves some of the leading brands in Education, Real Estate, and retail categories.

    With a team of creative maestros, BrandVita embarked on a journey to craft compelling narratives for brands, seamlessly blending innovation and strategy to leave an indelible mark in the hearts and minds of their target audience.

    Innovative
    approach

    BrandVita's unique approach to advertising was akin to weaving a tapestry of emotions and ideas, where each campaign was a carefully orchestrated symphony of visuals, words, and experiences designed to resonate with the diverse tastes of the modern consumer.

    In a world inundated with messages, BrandVita stood out as a beacon of creativity, consistently delivering campaigns that not only captured attention but also sparked conversations, fostering a genuine connection between brands and their consumers.

    As the chapters of BrandVita's success story unfolded, businesses across industries realized the transformative power of strategic storytelling, turning to the company as the trusted partner in their quest to elevate brand presence and carve a lasting legacy in the ever-evolving landscape of advertising and media.
