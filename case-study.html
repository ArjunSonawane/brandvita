    Challenges

    1. The ticket size of the property (Villas: Simplex & Duplex) was a little higher size due to its premium quality. So, we needed to focus the campaign at locations where a maximum of 4-wheeler owners / high-income groups are addressed.
    2. Since the campaign required a high density, the availability of multiple sites on the targetted location was a big challenge.

    Our strategy
    and approach

    1. We did a little bit of analytics (Descriptive analysis) from the existing data of ELDECO Imperia 1, and found out the:
      • most of the buyers were first-home buyers. They found it as an excellent investment with high appreciation due to Eldeco's brand equity, history of timely delivery, and the features of the property which included many factors.
      • most buyers had more than 2 lac
      • traveled by air frequently.
    1. As Imperia 2 is within 5km from the Airport, we identified the route converging to the airport and identified the routes considering the analysis done with the buyers of Imeperia
    2. Eldeco had a clear view of repeating "Imperia 1" success. So, they had the message: "Opportunity knocks again". We also synced.
    3. Started the hunt for OoH media with a larger format to create a big impact after shortlisting the routes.
    4. After shortlisting groundwork was done by collecting the GPS (longitude and Latitude coordinates) of each hoarding and measuring the distances from the property and the key locations.
    5. Ready with the ammunition, we launched the campaign with overnight execution.
    6. We had plotted all OoH on the map to see the campaign coverage.
    7. The campaign was highly successful with the property sold out within a month ( see the attached print advertisement)
